About

The Ari Nahum Trio will perform innovative interpretations of popular songs, originals, and classic jazz standards. Pianist Ari Nahum has been collaborating with bassist Jim Pacala for the past 17 years and will be joined by the sensitive, prominent Twin Cities drummer Ken Goldman.

Based in Minneapolis, MN, the Ari Nahum Trio brings together decades of musical experience and a shared love of adventurous, heartfelt jazz.

Ari Nahum

Piano

Ari Nahum began playing jazz while studying at Colorado College. His musical journey from there has been intertwined with medical studies as he explores the sounds that capture and encompass the human experience.

Jim Pacala

Bass

As a high schooler in Rochester, NY, Jim Pacala began playing gigs by hanging out with jazz cat students at the Eastman School of Music. His jazz playing has been informed by work in classical, bluegrass, roots, rock, and klezmer, while also intertwined, like Ari's, with medical studies.

Ken Goldman

Drums

Ken Goldman began his percussion studies in Iowa City and later focused on classical music at Northwestern University before shifting to drum set. Now based in Minneapolis, he performs with local groups and contributes to works spanning documentary film, songwriting, and collaborative performance projects.
